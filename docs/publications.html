Research themes

My published work spans three main areas:

Recreational fisheries
Survey design, harvest estimation, and CPUE standardisation for inshore finfish and rock lobster fisheries in South Africa and New Zealand.

Fish movement and behaviour
Acoustic telemetry studies on the movement of coastal reef fish species in southern Africa, including implications for MPA design and fisheries management.

Marine protected areas
Monitoring fish assemblages in MPAs, evaluating MPA effectiveness, and contributing to MPA network design processes.
